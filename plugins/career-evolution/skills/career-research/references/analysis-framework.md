# Analysis Framework

## The "So What" Rule
Every finding MUST answer: "What does this mean for Chris specifically?"

Raw finding alone is not useful. Connect it to Chris's profile, skills, and strategic paths.

## Strategic Path Lens

Evaluate each finding through both paths:

### Primary: AI-Augmented Engineering Leader
Merged from "Master AI Usage" + "Build AI Tools" (decision 2026-03-21). An engineer who builds custom AI tooling AND orchestrates AI workflows for teams.

- **Signals that matter:** AI tool maturity, productivity multipliers, enterprise AI adoption, MCP/agent ecosystem, developer tools market, team lead + AI workflow patterns, demand for AI infrastructure builders
- **Key question:** Does this make "AI-augmented engineering leader who builds tools" more or less viable?
- **Positive signals:** AI tools plateau (validates AGI bet), human-AI collaboration patterns emerge, premium on orchestration skills, growing MCP/agent ecosystem, enterprise AI tooling early-stage, new frameworks emerging
- **Negative signals:** AI becomes fully autonomous (no need for orchestrators), AI tool market consolidates (no room for builders), building AI tools itself gets automated, companies eliminate team lead roles

### Watchlist: Career Pivot
Kept as monitoring-only (decision 2026-03-21). Not actively pursued but tracked for signals.

- **Signals that matter:** Which fields are AI-resistant, what roles grow that leverage engineering thinking without pure coding
- **Key question:** Are there adjacent fields where Chris's skills transfer and AI amplifies rather than threatens?
- **Positive signals:** Growing demand in AI-adjacent fields (AI safety, technical product management, developer education, infrastructure architecture)
- **Negative signals:** Hard to find fields that value engineering background AND are AI-resistant

## Weighting

- **AI-Augmented Engineering Leader:** 70% weight (primary, active pursuit)
- **Career Pivot:** 30% weight (watchlist, monitoring only)
- Never drop the watchlist to 0% — surprises happen

## Connection to Profile

When analyzing:
1. Map finding to Chris's current skills (profile/skill-inventory.md)
2. Identify skill gaps the finding reveals
3. Check if finding affects any strategic bets
4. Consider if finding changes urgency of any existing recommendations
